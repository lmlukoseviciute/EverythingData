Data Extraction and EDA on WHO Tuberculosis Cases Dataset

Data extraction and merging from different data sources into one dataset. Exploratory data analysis on Tuberculosis cases dataset from World Health Organization.

Overview

I was seeking data on tuberculosis (TB) cases, along with information on each country's population size and income level, to conduct a thorough analysis. However, I couldn’t locate this exact dataset on the WHO website, so I decided to create my own solution. This project examines an aggregated TB dataset compiled from the WHO and World Bank, now available on Kaggle.

The goal of this project is to shed light on TB statistics that are often unfamiliar to the average Western audience—and to help change that perception. Tuberculosis is a completely treatable disease and could be eradicated, just like smallpox was. Yet, for reasons beyond medicine, we have chosen not to achieve this.

As John Green, author of Everything is Tuberculosis, insightfully noted:
“We have build systems that simply do not value all human lives in the same way. And that is a real failure on our part.”
